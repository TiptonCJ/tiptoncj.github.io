ADCC Data Analysis

This project is a data dashboard created in Tableau, showcasing the analysis of the ADCC (Abu Dhabi Combat Club) dataset from Kaggle.
The dashboard includes various visualizations that provide insights into the performance of fighters, match statistics, and trends over time.
Try clicking on different visualizations and adjusting filters to explore the data in depth.
As an experienced Jiu-Jitsu practitioner and former president of the Jiu-Jitsu club at my university, I found this dataset particularly interesting.
The dashboard allows for a detailed analysis of fight outcomes, techniques used, and the performance of different fighters over the entire history of ADCC up until 2022.

Some important insights to keep in mind while exploring the dashboard:
- The dataset includes information on over 1000 matches, with details on fighters, weight classes, and match outcomes.
- You can filter by fighter, gender, weight class, and submission to see specific trends and statistics.
- Please note that women's divisions weren't introduced until 2011 and only has two weight classes compared to the mens six classes.
- The absolute division is an open weight men's division that is only open to the top placing competitors in their respective divisions.
